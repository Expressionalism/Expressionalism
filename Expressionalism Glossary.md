## **Introduction**

This glossary provides a definitive, rigorous, and accessible reference for the terminology, concepts, and mathematical apparatus of Expressionalism. It draws directly from the Framework (P1–P8), the Toolkit, the Metric Validation Results (May 22, 2026), and the Objections document.

Expressionalism is a conditional, dialectical system that measures meaning as the living tension between connectable certainties (produced through secondary synthesis) and the uncertainties, generative pauses, and non-relational gaps that necessarily remain. Its fundamental ontological primitive is **pointing** — any provisional stabilization of secondary synthesis that registers as a primary expression.

---

## **How to Use This Glossary**

When running the toolkit, refer to Section 1 for toggle and phase definitions and Section 2 for the exact formulas behind every metric. When teaching or writing about Expressionalism, start with the plain-language summaries and move to the mathematical articulations only as needed. All entries remain explicitly provisional in keeping with P8’s open perception gate.

The system treats every objection, refinement, or use of this glossary itself as a new primary expression subject to the same P1–P8 analysis. The Open Field stays open.
